About Me

I’ve always been drawn to the arts—whether through creating DIY crafts, designing visuals, or expressing myself through music and singing. That love for creativity naturally extended into the digital world, where I discovered a passion for graphic design, web development, and working with data.

Along the way, I’ve learned to use tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, and Tableau. These skills have allowed me to build user-friendly websites, design thoughtful visuals, and transforming complex data into simple, meaningful stories through visualizations. What matters most to me is being able to create with purpose—whether it’s a clean, functional interface or a graphic that tells a story. I’m always eager to keep learning, stay curious, and do work that’s thoughtful and sincere.
